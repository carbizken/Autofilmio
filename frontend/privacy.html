Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

AutoFilm ("we," "us") provides video messaging software to automotive dealerships. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to autofilm.io, our applications, and messages sent through our platform on behalf of dealerships.

Information we collect

  • Dealership account data: names, work emails, phone numbers, and roles of dealership staff who use AutoFilm.
  • Customer contact data: when a dealership sends you a video, we process the name, phone number, and email the dealership provided, plus the vehicle of interest.
  • Engagement data: whether and how much of a video was watched, link clicks, and message delivery status. This is shared with the dealership that sent the message.
  • Technical data: IP address, device type, and browser information used for security, rate limiting, and analytics.

SMS / text messaging

Program description: Dealerships use AutoFilm to send personal video messages, service updates, and appointment information by SMS/MMS to customers who provided their phone number to the dealership.

Message frequency varies by your relationship with the dealership. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt out any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for help. Once you opt out, our systems block all further messages to your number across the platform.

We do not sell your phone number or messaging data. No mobile information is shared with third parties for their own marketing.

How we use information

  • To deliver videos, texts, and emails on behalf of your dealership
  • To show dealerships whether their messages were watched or answered
  • To operate, secure, and improve the platform
  • To comply with law, including TCPA and CTIA messaging rules

Sharing

We share data with: the dealership you're communicating with; our processors (video hosting, SMS carrier services, email delivery, payments, database hosting) strictly to run the service; and authorities when legally required. We never sell personal information.

Retention & deletion

Engagement records are retained while the dealership maintains its account, then deleted on schedule. To request deletion of your personal data, contact your dealership or email privacy@autofilm.io — we honor verified requests within 30 days.

Your rights

Depending on your state (including California), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information and to opt out of certain processing. Contact privacy@autofilm.io to exercise them.
